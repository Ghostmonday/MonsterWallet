Privacy Policy

Effective date: November 22, 2025

1. Information We Collect

  • Device identifier (UUID) – for push‑notification registration.
  • Public blockchain addresses you import – stored locally only.
  • Analytics events (optional, opt‑out in Settings) – sent to Firebase.

2. How We Use Your Information

We never sell or share personal data with third parties. All wallet keys remain on‑device and are never transmitted to our servers.

3. Data Retention

Data is retained only as long as you keep the app installed. Deleting the app removes all stored data.

4. Your Rights

You may request deletion of any analytics data by contacting support.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 22, 2025

1. Introduction

KryptoClaw ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

2. No Personal Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. KryptoClaw is a non-custodial wallet, meaning:

  • Your private keys are stored securely on your device's Secure Enclave.
  • We do not have access to your keys, funds, or transaction history.
  • We do not track your IP address or device identifiers.

3. Blockchain Data

When you transact on the blockchain, your transaction details (sender, receiver, amount) are publicly recorded on the blockchain ledger. This is inherent to how blockchains work and is outside our control.

4. Third-Party Services

Our app connects to public blockchain nodes (e.g., Ethereum, Bitcoin, and Solana endpoints) to fetch balances and broadcast transactions. These providers may see your IP address when a request is made, but we do not share any personal identifiers with them.

5. Biometric Data

We use Face ID or Touch ID to authenticate you locally on your device. This biometric data is processed entirely by the iOS operating system and is never accessible to our app or sent to any server.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@kryptoclaw.app.
